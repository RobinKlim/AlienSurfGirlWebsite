About

When Robin (Guitar), Philipp (Bass), Jonas (Vocals), Morten (Drums) and Jana (Keys) met in Hamburg in 2020, they fell musically in love with each other.

Within their songwriting process they are always chasing moments of creative flow.

Music

Surprisingly sparkling, unexpectedly fruity. Multi-layered taste. Always intense, but never too sweet. Light as a feather in the finish.

Click the play button to open a world so full of energy that it condenses on the outside. Not resealable. This experience wants to be savored to the last sip.
